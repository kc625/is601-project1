    Where Did The Internet Come From?

    If you asked the average person, I bet many would tell you that the internet is a pretty recent invention. Seems reasonable at first, no? After all, it is often associated with the turn of the millennium. While Y2K and the internet certainly have connections with one another, the internet actually has a much richer and much longer history than most people realize. In fact, the origins of the internet can actually be traced all the way back to World War II. Also, its inception is linked much more closely to the telephone than most people know.
